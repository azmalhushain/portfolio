OUR CLASSES

SUBJECTS

ENGLISH

Our English program for primary students is designed to develop their reading and writing skills. Students will learn to read and write basic words, sentences, and short stories. The program also focuses on developing their listening and speaking skills.

MATHEMATICS

Our mathematics program for primary students is designed to make math fun and interactive. Students will learn basic mathematical concepts such as numbers, counting, addition, subtraction, and shapes through hands-on activities and games.

COMPUTER

Our computer science program for primary students is designed to introduce them to the basics of technology and computer literacy. Students will learn basic computer skills such as typing, mouse navigation, and internet safety through fun and interactive activities.

URDU

Our Urdu program for primary students is designed to introduce them to the Urdu language and culture. Students will learn basic vocabulary, grammar, and sentence structure through interactive activities and games. They will also learn about Urdu literature and poetry.

MUSICS & DANCE

Our Music and Dance program for primary students is designed to introduce them to the world of music and dance in a fun and interactive way. Students will learn basic rhythm, beat, and movement through singing, dancing, and playing musical instruments.

SCIENCE

Our Science program for primary students is designed to introduce them to the world of science in an interactive and fun way. Students will learn about different topics such as plants, animals, and the five senses through hands-on experiments and activities.
